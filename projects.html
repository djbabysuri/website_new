Projects

Cleaning house...

System integration dysfunction... it happens. We're currently working with our client on their Salesforce, Action Network and ActBlue integrations to clean up the mess and get their platforms playing nicely with one another again.

On the move...

Sometimes the tools we're using are not the best tools for the job. In multifaceted organizations tools for one department might not be ideal for another. We're currently working with one of our clients to transition their fieldwork operations over from Salesforce to EveryAction, while their development and public affairs teams remain on Salesforce. The two CRM systems will be synced, allowing each team to use the platform that best facilitates their work.

Something different...

We're partnering up with one of our favorite schools to support in their Restorative and Transformative Justice practices. We've brought in a long-time RJ practitioner to support school staff with mediations and facilitations while the data squad focuses on building sustainable systems that can be used to track both short and long-term behavioral and school-wide culture shifts.
